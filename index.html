About

Teaching style

I focus on fundamentals, then build confidence with guided practice. We’ll turn confusion into a checklist of skills you can repeat under pressure.

What makes this different

You won’t just “get the answer.” You’ll learn how to recognize problem types, choose the right method, and avoid common traps.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you do online tutoring?

Yes. Online sessions use Zoom + an interactive whiteboard. It works great for math.

What should I bring?

Your notes, recent homework/tests, and the topics you’re struggling with. I’ll handle the structure.

How often should we meet?

Most students improve fastest with weekly sessions. For exams, 2–3 sessions/week can help.

Can you help with proofs?

Yes — we’ll focus on logic, structure, and writing clean solutions step-by-step.
